Server Rules

Please follow the rules below

Respect is mandatory

Treat everyone with respect. Discrimination, hate speech, racism, or harassment of any kind will not be tolerated.

No advertising

Do not advertise other servers, virtual airlines, or websites unless explicitly approved by an admin.

Use correct channels

Stay on topic by posting in the appropriate channels (e.g., flight logs, support, etc.).

Appropriate usernames

Use clear and professional usernames, preferably with your pilot ID (e.g., [VA123] John).

No spam or flooding

Avoid repeated messages, excessive tagging, or emoji spam.

Flight conduct

Follow airline rules (correct aircraft, routes). Fake logs or cheating is strictly prohibited.

Language

This is an English-speaking server. Please use English to ensure everyone can participate.

Voice chat etiquette

Be respectful and clear when speaking. Use Push-to-Talk to avoid background noise if needed.

Support & inquiries

Use the #support channel or open a ticket for any issues or questions.

Staff decisions are final

Staff are here to help. Please respect their actions and speak privately if needed.

Punishment system

1st degree: 1 month ban
2nd degree: 1 week ban
3rd degree: 24-hour ban
